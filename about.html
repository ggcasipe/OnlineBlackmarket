BlackMarket


About the Company

This is an online shopping site that aims to sell a variety of random things. The online shop is a parody of the infamous black market. It also uses the “cart system” where you can put all the things you want to buy in the cart, just like any other online shops. All items being sold at this online shop can only be paid using primogems.
